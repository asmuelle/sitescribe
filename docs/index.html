Straight answers

The questions field crews actually ask.

Does it work with no internet? +

Yes — fully offline. Photo OCR, barcode reads, voice transcription and report drafting all run on-device. SiteScribe needs zero bars to capture, draft, review and search your work.

When does it sync? +

Automatically, when coverage returns. Everything is saved locally first; the moment you're back in signal, drafts and finalized reports sync in the background — no manual upload step.

Can I trust the extracted data on a liability document? +

Every field shows its provenance (which photo or voice note it came from) and a confidence score. Nothing is finalized until you confirm or edit it — so the report you sign is the report you reviewed.

What can I export? +

Clean PDF for the client or claim file, and CSV for your records and billing systems. Both generate on-device and are ready the moment your draft is approved.

What does it read from a photo? +

Equipment tags, serial plates, model and meter values, barcodes, and receipts — mapped into your report schema as defect, location, severity and recommended action.
